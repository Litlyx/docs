## Why marketing in Litlyx is built on UTMs

Litlyx is designed around a simple idea: **you should understand your marketing performance without sacrificing privacy**.  
To do this, Litlyx leans on **UTM parameters** as the primary way to understand where traffic comes from and how campaigns perform.

Instead of following users across the web or building invasive profiles, Litlyx focuses on:

- **The link they clicked** (with UTMs attached)
- **The page they landed on**
- **What happened during that visit or session**

This gives you rich, actionable insights while keeping tracking **transparent, predictable, and respectful**.

---

## Why this is an ethical way of marketing in 2026

In 2026, privacy expectations and regulations are higher than ever:

- Browsers block many third-party cookies by default.
- Users are more aware of how their data is used.
- Regulations like **GDPR** and similar frameworks worldwide demand **data minimization** and **purpose limitation**.

Litlyx embraces this by:

- **Not relying on dark patterns or hidden tracking.**
- **Using UTMs as explicit signals** that you control in your campaigns.
- **Focusing on aggregated traffic and behavior**, not individual identities.

UTMs tell you:

- Which **source** and **medium** brought the user.
- Which **campaign** they belong to.
- Optionally, which **keyword** or **creative** they clicked.

Nothing about this needs to invade personal privacy. You see **how your marketing performs**, not **who a specific person is**. This is what makes UTM-driven analytics a strong foundation for **ethical marketing** in 2026.

---

## Why using 100% of your traffic data matters

Many legacy analytics setups only give you a partial view:

- Some users block third-party scripts entirely.
- Data is sampled, making reports fuzzy or delayed.
- Complex setups lead to broken tags and missing events.

Litlyx aims to work with **100% of the traffic it can lawfully and technically observe** on your site:

- The Litlyx script is **lightweight** and **first-party oriented**, helping it run reliably.
- When a visitor lands on your page and UTMs are present, **every one of those visits is eligible for tracking** (according to your configuration and consent model).
- You get **full-fidelity data**: no artificial sampling, and a clear picture of all the campaigns that send people to your pages.

This makes your reporting:

- **More accurate**: decisions are based on all available traffic, not on partial or sampled views.
- **More fair**: small but high-impact channels are visible, not lost in averages.
- **More trustworthy**: teams can rely on the numbers when allocating budget and planning growth.

## How this connects to UTMs in Litlyx

In Litlyx, UTMs are the bridge between:

- your **marketing work** (emails, ads, social posts, partnerships), and  
- your **analytics** (visits, sessions, conversions, product usage).

By consistently tagging your links with UTMs, you:

- Turn every campaign into a **measurable, comparable data stream**.
- Keep tracking **simple, explicit, and ethical**.
- Unlock the full power of Litlyx dashboards using **100% of the traffic data** you collect.
