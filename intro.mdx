## What you can do with it
Litlyx offers different insights that range from simple visitor tracking to advanced product analytics, all in a single dashboard. Here is a simple table that recaps all the features Litlyx offers:

| Feature                 | Description                                                                 |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `Visitors Tracking`      | Simply see how visitors interact with your site or app.                     |
| `Web Analytics`          | Real-time users, bounce rate, or understand where your audience finds you.  |
| `Ethical Marketing`      | Control parameters like UTM, Medium, and Types of sources with ease.        |
| `Product Analytics`      | Track custom actions with a level of freedom unheard of before.             |
| `Granular Permissions`   | Whether they are team members or clients, handle permissions for each of them. |
| `Customizable Reports`   | Create comprehensive reports with your own brand and logos.                 |
| `AI Data Analysis`       | Brainstorm on your data, ask for metrics, and let our built-in AI help you grow. |
