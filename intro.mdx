## What you can do with it
Litlyx gives you everything from simple visitor stats to advanced product analytics, all in one place. Here’s a quick overview:

| Feature   | Description                                           |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| `Visitors` | See who visits, from where, and on which pages.      |
| `Website`  | Track traffic, engagement, and key performance.      |
| `Marketing`| Measure campaigns, UTMs, and which channels convert. |
| `Product`  | Follow key actions users take inside your product.   |
| `Members`  | Invite teammates or clients with the right access.   |
| `Reports`  | Build and share simple, branded reports.            |
| `AI`       | Ask questions in chat and get clear, data-based tips.|


## Where are we going?
We are building a privacy-first, all-in-one platform that gives you a 360° view across web analytics, product, marketing, SEO, and how your brand ranks in LLMs... all of this accessible through an AI copilot that tells you what to change to improve your outcomes.
