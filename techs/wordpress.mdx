## Pre-Requisites

Before proceeding, ensure you:
<Steps>
<Step title="Configure your Wordpress Account">
    Get started [here](https://wordpress.org/documentation/article/get-started-with-wordpress/).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your litlyx's project ID">
    Sign up on [Litlyx Dashboard](https://dashboard.litlyx.com/) and create a new project.
  </Step>
</Steps>


## 1. Initialize

After activation, go to the `Litlyx Analytics` tab in the WordPress admin menu.

## 2. Obtain your project ID
Create a project on [Litlyx Cloud](https://dashboard.litlyx.com/)  and retrieve your project ID.

## 3. Setup the plugin

Under the voice `Instance type`, select `Default`. Enter your `Project ID` in the Litlyx settings to start tracking analytics immediately.  

## 4 Setup your custom events
To track custom events:
1. Assign a CSS class to any element on your website.
2. Open the Litlyx plugin tab in your WordPress dashboard.
3. Enter the CSS class, assign an event name, and optionally add metadata.


<Note>It works even with `Elementor`.</Note>




## 5. Your turn now!

That's it! Now you can go to your website and verify Litlyx Analytics script has been added and go to your dashboard to see whether the data are being tracked.
