## Pre-Requisites

Before getting started, make sure youâ€™ve signed up on [Litlyx](https://dashboard.litlyx.com/) and created a workspace.

## 1. Open Site Settings

Open Framer in your browser and select the project where you want to install Litlyx. Then, click on **Site Settings** from the top-left corner. Under the **General** tab, scroll down to the bottom of the section.

## 2. Add the Custom Code

Locate the section titled **Custom Code**. Paste the script provided by Litlyx (under the **Manual Installation** section, labeled *End of `<head>` tag*) into the appropriate field. Then click **Save**.

## 3. You're Done!

Litlyx is now successfully installed on your Framer project.
